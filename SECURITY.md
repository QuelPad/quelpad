# Security policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please email **security@quelpad.com**. A PGP key is published on
[quelpad.com](https://quelpad.com).

Do **not** open a public GitHub issue for security problems — issues here
are public immediately, before a fix can ship.

What helps us act fast:

- The QuelPad version (Settings → About) and your OS.
- Steps to reproduce, or a proof of concept.
- What data or boundary you believe is affected (credentials, script
  execution, license, update channel, …).

You'll get an acknowledgement, and a fix or a clear assessment as fast as a
one-person team can honestly deliver. Thank you for reporting responsibly.
